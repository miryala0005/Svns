Admissions 2025–26

Join the Sri Vidya Niketan family. Admissions are now open for Nursery through Class XII for the academic year 2025–26.

Simple & Transparent

Admission Process

Follow these four easy steps to secure your child's admission.

1

Enquiry & Form

Fill the online enquiry form below or visit the school office to collect a physical form.

2

Document Submission

Submit required documents at the school office. Verify the checklist below before visiting.

3

Interaction & Assessment

An age-appropriate interaction or assessment is conducted at the school campus.

4

Confirmation

Fee payment and confirmation of admission. Welcome kit is provided to all new students.
