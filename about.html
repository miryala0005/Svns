Our Story

A Legacy of
Educational Excellence

Founded in 1998 by a group of visionary educators, Sri Vidya Niketan School has grown from a small institution of 120 students to one of Hyderabad's most respected CBSE schools, today serving over 3,200 students from Nursery to Class XII.

Our philosophy centres on nurturing the complete individual — academic achievement, moral character, cultural pride, and creative thinking — equipping students to thrive in a dynamic global world.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

🎯

Our Mission

To provide every student with a high-quality, value-based education that fosters intellectual curiosity, ethical responsibility, and the confidence to contribute meaningfully to society. We strive to create an inclusive environment where diverse talents are recognized and nurtured.

🔭

Our Vision

To be the foremost institution in Hyderabad where students emerge as globally competitive, compassionate, and responsible citizens — equipped with the knowledge, skills, and values to lead in a rapidly evolving world while staying grounded in Indian cultural heritage.

Our Journey

School History

From humble beginnings to one of Hyderabad's premier schools.

1998

Foundation

Sri Vidya Niketan School founded by Mrs. Sarada Devi and a group of educators with 120 students and 12 staff members in Madhapur.

2005

CBSE Affiliation & Expansion

Received CBSE affiliation (No. 1300456) and expanded to Class X. New block constructed with 24 additional classrooms and a central library.

2012

Senior Secondary Wing

Class XI–XII Science and Commerce streams launched. State-of-the-art science labs inaugurated. Student strength crosses 1,500.

2020

Digital Transformation

All classrooms upgraded to smart classrooms. Online learning infrastructure built during the pandemic. Arts stream added in Senior Secondary.

2024

Silver Jubilee

Celebrating 25+ years of excellence with 3,200+ students, 150+ faculty, and a 98% board pass rate. New sports complex and innovation lab inaugurated.
