Academic Programmes

A rigorous, balanced CBSE curriculum from Nursery through Class XII — designed to challenge, inspire, and prepare every student.

🏆 2024 Board Results — 98.4% Pass Rate  |  12 Students scored 95%+  |  School Topper: Aakash Sharma — 98.8%

Curriculum

Classes Nursery to XII

Explore each wing's academic focus, subjects, and key learning outcomes.

Primary School – Classes I to V

Our primary programme lays the foundation for lifelong learning. We emphasise conceptual understanding through activity-based methods, play-way techniques, and a nurturing environment that builds curiosity and confidence.

Core Subjects

English Language Hindi Telugu Mathematics Environmental Studies (EVS) General Knowledge Computer Basics Art & Craft Physical Education Value Education

Key Focus Areas

  • ✦Activity-based and experiential learning approaches
  • ✦Reading, writing, and numeracy skill development
  • ✦Multilingual proficiency (English, Hindi, Telugu)
  • ✦Social skills, team building, and emotional intelligence
  • ✦Introduction to digital literacy and basic computing

Middle School – Classes VI to VIII

The middle school years are transformative. We deepen subject knowledge, introduce critical thinking, and offer co-curricular opportunities in science clubs, debate, and sports to build well-rounded personalities.

Core Subjects

English Hindi / Telugu Mathematics Science Social Science Computer Science Sanskrit (Optional) Physical Education Art Education Work Education

Key Focus Areas

  • ✦Analytical and logical thinking across all subjects
  • ✦Lab experiments and project-based science learning
  • ✦Debate, public speaking, and leadership development
  • ✦Introduction to competitive mathematics (Olympiads)

Secondary School – Classes IX & X

Classes IX and X prepare students for the CBSE Board examinations with intensive academics, frequent practice tests, and career orientation programmes. Our track record of exceptional board results speaks for itself.

Core Subjects

English (Core) Hindi / Telugu Mathematics (Standard/Basic) Science Social Science Information Technology Physical Education Art Education

Key Focus Areas

  • ✦CBSE Board exam preparation with full-length mock tests
  • ✦Career counselling and stream selection guidance (Class X)
  • ✦Chapter-wise assignments and periodic revision tests
  • ✦Individual academic counselling sessions with subject teachers

Senior Secondary – Classes XI & XII

We offer three streams at the Senior Secondary level, each designed to align with students' career aspirations and provide a strong foundation for competitive entrance examinations (JEE, NEET, CA, CUET).

🔬 Science Stream

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Mathematics / Biology
  • English (Core)
  • Computer Science / IP
  • Physical Education (Elective)

JEE / NEET coaching integrated with school curriculum. Dedicated doubt-clearing sessions on weekends.

📊 Commerce Stream

  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • English (Core)
  • Mathematics / IP
  • Entrepreneurship (Elective)

Industry exposure through case studies, business fairs, and CUET/CA foundation preparation support.

🎨 Arts / Humanities

  • History
  • Political Science
  • Economics
  • English (Core)
  • Psychology / Sociology
  • Fine Arts (Elective)

Debate, MUNs, and social internships woven into the curriculum for real-world learning.
